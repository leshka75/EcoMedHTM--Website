Environmental Stewardship & Philanthropy

Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability are no longer "buzzwords". These principles are pivotal for long-term profitability and success. Our products, services and partners support our vision and commitment to environment. We have virtualized many operations to reduce our carbon footprint and implemented "green program execution" by following conservation strategies that include usage of "eco-friendly" cleansing solutions, re-utilization of testing tools and ecological disposition of scrap, batteries and heavy metals.

Our flagship headquarters are equipped with conservation technology to reduce the use of vital resources. In additional to eco-friendly infrastructure, we utilize energy-sustainable lighting, energy efficient A/C system and Eniscope, a real-time energy usage analyzing system, courtesy of Ensol Energy Management Solutions.

In addition to supporting sound environmental practices, we at EcoMed believe we have a duty of social responsibility. This philosophy motivates us to partner with our customers and enable them to support their missions. Throughout the year, we work with multiple Charitable Foundations on various projects and fundraisers

Our goal is to assist with resources and funding that will provide access to better technology, improve quality of care and promote healing. Charitable Foundation of every facility that either engages in our services or acquires our products, receives back a portion from every sale through our annual donation and gifting programs.
