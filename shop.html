Equipment Wish List

Our Equipment Wish List program is easy, complimentary and free of any commitment. You tell us of your medical equipment needs including condition (new or pre-owned), quantity, budget and acquisition timeline. Once such equipment becomes available, our sales team will put it on "hold" and will contact you with technical specs and details.

If you choose to purchase the equipment, we will assist you with the sale process. If your needs have changed and the equipment is no longer desired, we will release the "hold" and make it available to the rest of our customers and the general market.

To create an Equipment Wish List, submit your requirements to sales@ecomedhtm.com
