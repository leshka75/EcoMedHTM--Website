Storage Solutions and Logistics

Organic growth, expansion and contractions are frequent components of any business life cycle. When this cycle reaches its peak, real estate space becomes a high commodity and health care facilities may face a challenge of where to temporarily store medical equipment.

To remedy this situation, we offer logistics and temporary storage solutions. Our logistics partners expertly handle all types of medical and imaging equipment and provide door-to-door and white glove delivery services. They are capable of accommodating both, domestic and international shipments on a moment's notice and provide support through shipment optimization, central call centers and transportation management.

Our strategic alliances enable us to provide nationwide warehouse storage and management. Through use of cutting edge technology, we automate inventory management and provide accurate up-to-date view of inventory levels. We offer flexible terms with no minimum storage time and delivery prioritization when medical equipment is ready to be returned to the facility.
