Medical Equipment Purchasing and Planning

EcoMed HTM, LLC provides comprehensive medical equipment purchasing and planning services. Our innovative procurement strategies yield cost-savings, operating efficiencies and overall reduced capital expenditures.

Maintaining a vendor-independent platform enables us to work with multiple distributors and manufacturers of medical equipment and medical office furnishings. Our technical knowledge and "hands-on" experience of working with both, medical and imaging equipment enables us to validate functionality, operability and overall product quality.

In addition to being able to procure both, quality pre-owned and brand new medical equipment, we provide project management for facilities undergoing expansions or renovations. For such projects our deliverables include

  • Identification of equipment needs
  • Validation of equipment specs, confirmation of warranties, ensuring all pre-owned equipment meets FDA standards & manufacturer's guidelines
  • Provide overall savings from OEM list prices through utilization of proprietary sourcing strategies, group volume plans and leveraging of supplier contracts
  • Collection, verification and staging of purchased equipment in central location prior to site delivery
  • Arranging all logistics including freight and warehousing
  • Assembly and installation services
