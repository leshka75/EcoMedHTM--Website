EcoMed HTM - Medical Equipment Management & Remarketing

EcoMed HTM, LLC is a vendor-independent medical equipment management company. We provide equipment planning and remarketing services, end-of-use equipment management and storage solutions.

Our customized approach delivers strong financial returns and removes complexity and risk associated with disposal of surplus medical equipment. We understand the importance of generating commercial value for redundant medical equipment. Through our established Medical Assets Remarketing Services (MARS) we offer industry expertise and contacts required to generate resale prices above the market average, thus ensuring you obtain maximum return on your surplus equipment, without any retained liability.

Adopting a vendor-independent platform enables us to fulfill any type of purchase order. We distribute and support medical equipment and furniture from major manufacturers and retailers. Our procurement strategies are based on utilization methodology, failure-rate analysis and group volume discounts. This approach enables us to provide high-quality refurbished and brand new medical equipment as well as medical office furnishings at great values.

The key to our success is due to decades of technical training and working on medical and imaging equipment, project management expertise and strategic alliances.
