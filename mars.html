MARS - Medical Assets Remarketing Services

Multiple options are available for disposition of surplus, redundant and under-utilized medical assets. There are auctions, resale websites, dealer trade-in's, direct vendor sales and the list goes on. While each disposition method achieves the desired goal - removal of equipment from the facilities, the biggest common denominator all these disposition methods share are lack of transparency, hefty fees and ultimately reduced cash back to the equipment owner. Additionally, health care facilities that commonly utilize these disposition options dedicate extra resources in the form of personnel and time to oversee the disposition process.

In order to alleviate the burden health care facilities face from finding the time to manage disposition process, working with multiple vendors, researching value of equipment and maintaining disposition records, we have developed a method that is transparent, measurable and proven to yield the greatest financial returns.

We provide all the necessary resources for a successful disposition program implementation which include

  • Equipment inspection & evaluation
  • Ownership verification
  • Inventory and cataloguing of equipment, components & peripherals
  • Market value assessment based on condition & current market demand
  • Transportation & storage solutions
  • EPA, OSHA & HIPA compliance prior to disposition
  • Active Sale strategies and direct marketing services
  • Financial reporting
