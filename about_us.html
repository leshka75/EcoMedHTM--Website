About Us

EcoMed HTM, LLC is an independent medical equipment management company. We provide equipment life-cycle planning, management and storage solutions.

EcoMed is a collaboration between hospital supply chain, biomed and IT executives with direct knowledge and practical experience of capital equipment planning and budgeting processes. This partnership was created to alleviate challenges faced by health care facilities and provide real, measurable solutions.
