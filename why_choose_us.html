why choose us?

Statistically, Hospital Supply Chains spend approximately $8.0 billion each year on inventory management out of $11.5 billion in total supply chain costs. In this context even small percentage improvements quickly add up!

Active inventory management through disposition, can help hospitals reduce their costs by up to 3.5% within the first 4 years of operations.

So what does EcoMed has to do with this?

We provide resources and expertise to effectively manage equipment disposition. One of the primary benefits in utilizing our services is cost reduction. Traditional in-house inventory management processes require time, FTE labor, and real estate plus the associated financial expenses.

We have extensive expertise in working with medical equipment in both, hospital and non-hospital settings. Our team provides all the necessary resources to ensure equipment is handled properly. Our primary services revolve around establishing Fair Market Price based on demand & utilization. We strategically market and advertise medical equipment to a roster of targeted buyers that seek those particular assets.

Our business model and vendor-independent platform enabled us to form many strategic alliances with manufacturers, retailers and industry partners. These bi-lateral relationships resulted in preferred rates for products and services carried by our affiliates. With the forefront for maintaining lean operations and customer-focused philosophy we are able to pass along significant savings to our customer.
